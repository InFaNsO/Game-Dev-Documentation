
### Main Goal for Player

The player wakes from cryo-sleep as **the last carrier of pre-Breach knowledge** — a descendant of the [[14 Naming Glossary#The Federation|Mani Accord]] preserved through the long dark age after [[12 The Akashic and The Bleed#1 The Truth (revealed mid-game)|The Breach]]. The world remembers Mani as folklore; no one alive can refine it. The player's role:

- **Teach** — restore Mani refinement knowledge to the Grinders, beginning with **Bhu-Mani**
- **Reclaim** — re-activate the three [[05 Mega Structures - Game Worlds|Mega Structures]] in sequence: Lithic Mow → Genesis Vats → Prism Forge
- **Recover** — purify named Husk NPCs to begin rebuilding the lost species (Amphibians, Reptiles)
- **Choose** — at endgame, decide whether to rebuild civilization as humans-only (bad ending) or as a multi-species federation reborn (good ending)

> Detailed breakdown in [[13 Campaign Structure]].

### What the Player is NOT Trying to Do

- **Not** recovering the original Akash-Mani crystal — it was destroyed at The Breach
- **Not** sealing The Breach — already closed for millennia
- **Not** fighting an active cosmic-horror antagonist — the threat is historical, the consequences are present
- **Not** chasing a final boss — the climax is the moral Choice, not a battle
