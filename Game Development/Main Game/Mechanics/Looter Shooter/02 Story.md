
### Story Summary

You wake from cryo-sleep at the [[14 Naming Glossary#World Locations|Headwaters]] — a forgotten research outpost of the [[14 Naming Glossary#The Federation|Mani Accord]], the pre-Breach federation that built the three Mega Structures and refined [[14 Naming Glossary#The Substance|Mani]] for the world. The Accord is gone. Civilization collapsed millennia ago in [[12 The Akashic and The Bleed#2 Timeline|The Breach]] — a few catastrophic seconds when the Accord's secret experiment to open a doorway to **Akash** went wrong. The doorway collapsed. **The Bleed** poured through. The Amphibian and Reptile species were corrupted into immortal **Husks**. The knowledge of Mani refinement was lost.

You carry fragmentary memory of that knowledge. The current barbaric survivors — [[14 Naming Glossary#The Factions (current era)|the Grinders]] — use raw Mani as wild grenades, killing themselves with their own ignorance. **You can teach them.** What follows is a journey downriver to reclaim the three Mega Structures, refine the four elemental Manis (Bhu, Jal, Vayu, Agni), and face the Accord's hidden truth — revealed when you first purify a named Husk and learn what really happened at The Breach. At the end of the river you return to your base, gather the allies you've recruited, and choose what kind of civilization to rebuild.

### Main Quest

1. **Wake** at the Headwaters cryo facility. Discover the world's state. Encounter the first Grinder.
2. **Earn Grinder trust** through demonstration and shared work ([[15 Grinder Trust Arc]]). Teach the first Bhu-Mani refinement.
3. **Reclaim Lithic Mow** alongside the Grinders. Establish player base.
4. **Travel downriver to Genesis Vats.** Reclaim it in phases. Refine Jal-Mani and Vayu-Mani.
5. **Purify the first named Amphibian Husk** — the Accord's secret is revealed. The conspiracy reframes everything.
6. **Travel onward to Prism Forge.** Reclaim. Refine Agni-Mani. Purify a named Reptile Husk.
7. **Return to base.** Gather all allies. **The Choice** — humans-only (bad ending) vs. multi-species cooperation (good ending).

### Side Quests

- Learn the history of the world through Accord audio logs and purified-NPC dialogue
- Discover and recruit other cryo-sleeper survivors along the river
- Help the Grinders' splinter feuds, lost-hunter searches, and old-cache investigations
- Investigate Bleed-pooled hotspots for hidden lore fragments

### The Truth (mid-game reveal)

> Detailed in [[12 The Akashic and The Bleed#1 The Truth (revealed mid-game)|the cosmic-horror lore document]].

The Mani Accord did not destroy itself by accident. The inner circle was using refined Akash-Mani to open a **doorway to the dimension Akash** — the source-realm Akash-Mani is a sample of. The working castes (most Amphibians, most Reptiles, the common humans) did not know. The doorway opened. Something on the other side pushed back. The Breach was the result. The original Akash-Mani was destroyed in the same moment. **No Akash-Mani exists in the present world. The Accord's secret died with them — until the first purified Husk speaks.**
