
# Naming Glossary

Quick reference for all locked names in the looter shooter / main game lore. Sanskrit-rooted vocabulary with pronunciation guides for development reference and player-facing consistency.

---

## The Substance

| Name | Tier | Pronunciation | Meaning |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Mani** | Common (gameplay, UI) | MAH-nee | Jewel, gem, precious stone (Sanskrit मणि) |
| **Panchamani** | Formal (lore, audio logs) | PAN-cha-MAH-nee | "Five-Jewel" — the substance that contains all five elements |

### Refined Elemental Variants

| Name | Element | Pronunciation | Mega Structure Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Bhu-Mani** | Earth | BHOO-MAH-nee | Lithic Mow |
| **Jal-Mani** | Water | JAL-MAH-nee | Genesis Vats |
| **Vayu-Mani** | Air | VAH-yoo-MAH-nee | Genesis Vats |
| **Agni-Mani** | Fire | AHG-nee-MAH-nee | Prism Forge |
| **Akash-Mani** | Ether / Space | AH-kahsh-MAH-nee | **Destroyed in The Breach. Historical only. Never appears in the campaign.** |

---

## The Federation

| Name | Pronunciation | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **The Mani Accord** | (English) | The pre-Breach federation that built the three Mega Structures. Multi-species: Humans, Amphibians, Reptiles. Pragmatic-transactional alliance, not utopian harmony. |

---

## The Catastrophe

| Name | Pronunciation | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **The Breach** | (English) | The catastrophic event that ended the Mani Accord. **Lasted only seconds.** Caused by the Accord's secret attempt to refine Akash-Mani as a doorway to **Akash** (the source dimension). The doorway collapsed in on itself, leaking interdimensional radiation, then sealed. |

---

## The Source Dimension

| Name | Pronunciation | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Akash** | AH-kahsh | The dimension/realm from which Akash-Mani was a crystallized sample. Where the Accord tried to travel. Inhabitants ambiguous — never seen, never confirmed, never reachable. |

---

## The Corruption / Radiation

| Name | Tier | Pronunciation | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **The Akashic** | Formal (Accord audio logs, lore) | ah-KAH-shik | Interdimensional radiation that leaked through during The Breach. Permanently contaminated all Mani in the world, infused into the corrupted, pools in concentrated zones. |
| **The Bleed** | Common (survivors, gameplay) | (English) | What survivors call The Akashic. Pairs with The Breach — *"The Breach is the wound. The Bleed is what comes through."* Used in Bleed-touched, Bleed Vapor, Bleed Zone, Bleed-Husk. |

**Important:** The Bleed is **historical, not active.** The Breach has been sealed for millennia. The Bleed exists as permanent contamination in the world (in Mani, in Husks, in pooled zones) but no new radiation is leaking in.

## The Species

| Name | Role | Status |
|---|---|---|
| **Humans** | Pre-Breach: Accord coordinators/designers. Bio-neutral to Mani. | Mostly survived. Degraded into Grinders. Player is a cryo-survivor. |
| **Amphibians** | Pre-Breach: Vapor refiners at Genesis Vats. Bio-attuned to Jal/Vayu. | Corrupted en masse into immortal Husks. |
| **Reptiles** | Pre-Breach: Heat refiners at Prism Forge. Bio-attuned to Agni. | Corrupted en masse into immortal Husks. |

## The Factions (current era)

| Name | Disposition | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **The Grinders** | Semi-hostile → trustable | Sole human faction. ~15–20 hunter-gatherer descendants in Lithic Mow valley. Element-ignorant, mining-proficient. |
| **Amphibian Husks** | Hostile until biome reclaimed | Inhabit Genesis Vats. Immortal, since The Breach. |
| **Reptile Husks** | Hostile until biome reclaimed | Inhabit Prism Forge. Immortal, since The Breach. |
| **Rogue Grinder Defector** | Friendly | Single vendor NPC at player base. (Prototype only — likely folds into the main-tribe vendor in main game.) |

## The Mega Structures

| Name | Pronunciation | Role | Biome |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Lithic Mow** | (English) | Mines / refines **Bhu-Mani** | Mountain valley (north). Player starts here. |
| **Genesis Vats** | (English) | Refines **Jal-Mani + Vayu-Mani** via vapor + water | Massive lake (mid-river). Amphibian Husk territory. |
| **Prism Forge** | (English) | Refines **Agni-Mani** via lasers + sun heat | Desert (south, end of river). Reptile Husk territory. **Pipes heat back to Genesis Vats** in pre-Breach refinement chain. |

**The Old Capital does NOT exist as a fourth location.** Originally proposed, dropped during design. There is no Akash Mani relic to recover — the original was destroyed at The Breach.

## Sample lore one-liner

> *"Before The Breach, the three species of the Mani Accord refined the Panchamani for what they claimed was energy — but secretly, for a doorway to Akash. Something on the other side pushed back. The doorway collapsed, The Bleed leaked through, and the Husks have endured ever since. You wake to teach the survivors what was forgotten — and to choose what comes next."*
